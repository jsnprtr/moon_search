Privacy Policy

How do we use cookies?

We use the following cookies:

• Analytical/performance cookies. These cookies collect anonymous information on how people use our Platform. These are used for statistical purposes, they don’t contain personal information. They allow us to recognise and count the number of visitors to our Platform and to see how visitors move around our Platform when they are using it. This helps us to improve the way our Platform works, for example, by gathering data about visits to the website, i.e. number of visitors, length of time spent on a page or where visitors have come from. These cookies help you find what you are looking for easier – and make your experience much smoother.
